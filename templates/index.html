MediChain

Your secure, blockchain-powered platform for storing medical and payment records.

What We Do

MediChain empowers patients and providers by storing medical visit information, medications, allergies, and payment records securely on a blockchain. Your health data is private, tamper-proof, and always accessible.

Why It Matters

We believe healthcare records should belong to you. MediChain ensures transparency, security, and ownership in managing your medical history.

How It Works

  1. Register: Create an account with your email and a secure password.
  2. Log In: Access your secure MediChain dashboard.
  3. Add Records: Upload visit files and enter details like medications and allergies.
  4. OCR Preview: Extract and preview text from scanned files to auto-fill forms.
  5. Get Rewarded: Earn MDC tokens for each successful upload.
  6. Track History: View or export your blockchain-stored records anytime.
  7. Wallet Access: Check your MDC balance and transaction history.
